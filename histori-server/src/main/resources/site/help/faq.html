What is it histori.city?

histori.city is a maps app with a time slider

Histori.city is a maps application with a time slider. Just like a regular map app, you can zoom in to focus on a small patch of land, or zoom out to view a whole continent. Unlike most other map apps, you can also zoom around in TIME. You can zoom down to a examine events on a single day, or zoom out to a millenium to look at broader trends.

Although the current version does not yet allow user-contributed edits, the long-term vision is for most of the content on histori.city to be crowd-sourced, much like Wikipedia.
